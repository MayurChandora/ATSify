About ATSify

Empowering job seekers to beat the algorithms.

Applying for jobs today can feel like shouting into the void. You spend hours tailoring your resume, only for it to be instantly rejected by an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) before a human ever sees it.

ATSify was built to solve exactly this problem. We are dedicated to providing fresh graduates, junior developers, and career switchers with the tools they need to optimize their resumes for ATS algorithms.

By leveraging advanced AI capabilities through the Groq API and LLaMA 3 models, ATSify analyzes your resume against any job description in seconds. We give you a clear match score, highlight crucial missing keywords, and provide actionable suggestions to improve your chances of securing that initial interview.

Our Mission

To democratize the job application process by making ATS optimization accessible, fast, and highly accurate for everyone.

Whether you're applying for your first internship or transitioning into a new career path, ATSify is here to ensure your resume gets the attention it deserves.
